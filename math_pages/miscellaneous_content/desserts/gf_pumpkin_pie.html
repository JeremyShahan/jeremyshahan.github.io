Gluten Free Pumpkin Pie

Image

Fit

Description

Pumpkin pie is a dessert pie with a spiced, pumpkin-based custard filling. The pumpkin and pumpkin pie are both a symbol of harvest time, and pumpkin pie is generally eaten during the fall and early winter. In the United States and Canada it is usually prepared for Thanksgiving, Christmas, and other occasions when pumpkin is in season.

Pie Crust

Ingredients

3 and 3/4 cups of Better Batter gluten free flour 3 sticks unsalted butter 3/4 teaspoons of salt 3 tablespoons of sugar
1 and 1/2 teaspoons of apple cider vinegar 3 medium eggs

Tools

pie plate rolling pin parchment paper large bowl
rubber spatula fork 3/4 cup full tablespoon
3/4 teaspoon

Instructions

Dough Recipe

  • In a large bowl add in 3 and 3/4 cups of Better Batter gluten free flour.
  • Add in all sticks of the butter by cutting it into the bowl.
  • Mix until the dough resembles a crumbly texture (the size of small peas).
  • Add in the 3/4 teaspoons of salt, the 3 tablespoons of sugar, the 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of apple cider vinegar, and the 2 eggs.
  • Stir to mix in the ingredients.
  • Work the dough with your hands until it comes together to form a loose, large ball. It should stick together pretty easily.
  • If it does not stick together, add 1-2 teaspoons water and work the dough some more.
  • Divide the dough into 2 equal parts and form each into a ball.
  • Place each dough ball on a large sheet of parchment paper and fold the paper over and press each ball into a disc.
  • Wrap up both discs and place it in the refrigerator. Allow the dough to chill for 1-2 hours.
  • If the dough has been chilled for longer than 2 hours, let it come to room temperature for 30-60 minutes before rolling it out.

Rolling out the Dough

  • Remove the dough from the parchment paper and dust the paper lightly with flour. Place the dough back onto the parchment paper.
  • Dust the top of the dough lightly with flour and add another sheet of parchment paper on top.
  • Using a rolling pin, roll the dough into a 1/8 to a 1/4 inch thick round crust. If the dough starts to stick to the parchment paper, repeat the light dusting process with flour.
  • Carefully remove the top parchment paper. Slide your hand under the bottom layer of the parchment paper and carefully flip the dough over into the pie plate.
  • Carefully remove the remaining parchment paper from the dough. Gently press the dough into the pie plate, trim the excess dough from the edges, and leave enough to create a thick edge. Fill in the thinner spots as needed with the excess dough.
  • Add a decorative pattern to the edge if desired.
  • Press a fork 5-6 times into the bottom of the pie crust. This will help the pie crust stay flat and not bubble up when baking or pre-baking.

Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

1 unbaked pie crust 30 ounces of pumpkin puree 28 ounces of sweetened condensed milk 4 medium eggs
2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon of ground ginger 1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg 1 teaspoon of salt
4 teaspoons of Better Batter gluten free flour

Tools

pie plate whisk parchment paper large bowl
parchment paper rubber spatula fork 3/4 cup
full tablespoon 3/4 teaspoon

Instructions

  • Place oven rack in the center position and Preheat the oven to 390°F (199°C).
  • Remove the pie crust dough from the fridge and let rest at room temperature for 20-30 minutes.
  • In a large bowl mix in the 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon, 1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg, 1 teaspoon of ginger, and 1 teaspoon of salt.
  • Also add in the 4 eggs, 30 ounces of pumpkin puree, and 28 ounces of sweetened condensed milk into the large bowl. Mix well
  • On a lightly floured surface, roll one disc into a 15 inch circle that is 1/8 inches thick. Carefully lay the crust into the bottom of a deep dish pie plate.
  • Pour the pumpkin pie filling into the crust.
  • Use a sharp knife to trim the dough along the outside edge of the pie plate.
  • Bake at 390°F (199°C) for 45 minutes. Turn the oven down to 375° and continue to bake for an additional 40-45 minutes or until the edge of the crust is golden brown. Cool at room temperature for around 3 hours.
