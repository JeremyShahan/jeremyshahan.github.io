Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is a dessert pie with a spiced, pumpkin-based custard filling. The pumpkin and pumpkin pie are both a symbol of harvest time, and pumpkin pie is generally eaten during the fall and early winter. In the United States and Canada it is usually prepared for Thanksgiving, Christmas, and other occasions when pumpkin is in season.

Ingredients

1 unbaked pie crust 30 ounces of pumpkin puree 28 ounces of sweetened condensed milk
4 medium eggs 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg 1 teaspoon of salt 4 teaspoons of Better Batter gluten free flour

Tools

pie plate whisk parchment paper large bowl
parchment paper rubber spatula fork 3/4 cup
full tablespoon 3/4 teaspoon

Instructions

  • Place oven rack in the center position and Preheat the oven to 390°F (199°C).
  • Remove the pie crust dough from the fridge and let rest at room temperature for 20-30 minutes.
  • In a large bowl mix in the 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon, 1 teaspoon of ground nutmeg, 1 teaspoon of ginger, and 1 teaspoon of salt.
  • Also add in the 4 eggs, 30 ounces of pumpkin puree, and 28 ounces of sweetened condensed milk into the large bowl. Mix well
  • On a lightly floured surface, roll one disc into a 15 inch circle that is 1/8 inches thick. Carefully lay the crust into the bottom of a deep dish pie plate.
  • Pour the pumpkin pie filling into the crust.
  • Use a sharp knife to trim the dough along the outside edge of the pie plate.
  • Bake at 390°F (199°C) for 45 minutes. Turn the oven down to 375° and continue to bake for an additional 40-45 minutes or until the edge of the crust is golden brown. Cool at room temperature for around 3 hours.
