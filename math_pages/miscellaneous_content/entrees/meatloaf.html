Meatloaf Recipe

Image

Fit

Description

Meatloaf is a baked dish of ground meat that's often mixed with other ingredients and molded into a loaf shape. It's usually made with ground beef, but can also be made with ground lamb, pork, veal, venison, poultry, or seafood. Other ingredients that might be added include breadcrumbs, seasonings, egg, and vegetables. Once baked, meatloaf can be sliced into individual portions like bread.

Ingredients

1 and 1/4 cups of onion (chopped) 1 and 1/4 cups of portobello mushrooms (chopped) 1/2 cup of celery (finely chopped)
1/2 a teaspoon of dried thyme 1 teaspoon of garlic (finely chopped) 1/2 cup of grated carrots
1 cup of fresh bread crumbs 1/2 cup of heavy cream 2 eggs
1 and 1/2 pounds ground chuck 1/2 pounds ground pork 4 slices of bacon
2 teaspoons of kosher salt 1/2 teaspoon of ground pepper 1/2 cup of ketchup
2 tablespoons of brown sugar 2 teaspoons of apple cider vinegar

Tools

cutting board knife medium pan solid spatula
whisk 2 small bowls large bowl full teaspoon
1/2 teaspoon full tablespoon full cup 1/2 cup
1/4 cup

Instructions

Vegetable Mixture

  • Add 1 and 1/4 cups of chopped onion, 1 and 1/4 cups of chopped mushroom and 1/2 a cup of finely chopped celery to medium pan. Season to taste with kosher salt and pepper.
  • Sprinkle 1/2 a teaspoon of thyme over onion mixture.
  • Rub 1 teaspoon of garlic and pinch of salt into a paste; add garlic to pan.
  • Cook and stir until onions are tender and translucent. Stir in 1/2 a cup of grated carrots; remove from heat. Cool completely.

Breadcrumb Mixture

  • Soak 1 cup of bread crumbs in 1/2 a cup of heavy cream in a small bowl.
  • Add in 2 eggs and lightly beat them into the mixture.

Ketchup Topping

  • Mix 1/2 a cup of ketchup, 2 tablespoons of brown sugar and 2 teaspoons of apple cider vinegar in a small bowl.

Meatloaf

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • In a large mixing bowl combine 1 and 1/2 pounds of ground chuck, 1/2 a pound of ground pork, 4 slices of bacon, cooled vegetables and bread crumbs mixture.
  • Sprinkle in 2 teaspoons of salt and 1/2 a teaspoon of ground pepper. Using hands, mix until well blended.
  • Place the meatloaf mixture in a 9" by 5" loaf pan.
  • Spoon the ketchup topping over the meatloaf mixture.
  • Bake on middle rack for 1 hour or until meat thermometer reaches 155 degree F. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing.
